# The Bluecoat School Liverpool's PA Consulting Raspberry Pi competition 2026 Entry

## The purpose:
- protect our elderly with our carefully designed IRDAHA (Image Recognition Detection And Help Awareness) technology
- we can *recognise* an elderly person in the cameras field of view, *detect* if they are in danger, then *help* them - all while being *aware* of everything else that is happening in frame
- if an elderly person has a dangerous fall in their care home, they can end up not being found for hours, some cases show up to 4 hours of wait time until the patient was found.
- this is ***not*** ok.
- whilst it can be for perfectly valid reasons, such as staff being busy, or even ill themlselfs, there must be a solution.
- there ***is***, and ***we*** made it.

## the solution
- small rovers, that circuit each floor, in order to discover unresponsive bodies faster, more efficiently - and while giving our nurses a break so they can do more usefull tasks, like checking up on patients individually.
    

- security cameras have blindspots, but if they move, then those blindspots will not be there, as all corners can be covered by small robots moving in cycles accross all floors of a building.
